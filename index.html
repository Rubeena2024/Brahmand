THE UNIVERSE
यत्र नान्तो न चादिस्तंस्तत्र केवलमस्तितम्।
आत्मानं न विजानीयादादौ च जगदात्मनः॥

In that place where there is no end, no beginning and only the self exists,
one should realize that the universe is a manifestation of the self.
